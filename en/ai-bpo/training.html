Why us

We're not a training company.
We rebuilt a broadcaster with AI — ourselves.

We walked into Radio Osaka (OBC) — a broadcaster we invested in — and eliminated every paper voucher and approval stamp. 2,500–3,000 journal entries a month, processed automatically by AI. We deployed Benten Obi as an AI announcer and launched Daily Radio Osaka, an AI-powered broadcast summary. We rebuilt a broadcaster, end to end, with AI.

We didn't learn "what AI can do" from a textbook. We did it with our own hands. That's why we can teach what actually works.
