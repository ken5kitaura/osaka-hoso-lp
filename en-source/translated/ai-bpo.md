### [section #faq] FAQ
- **eyebrow**: FAQ

FAQ

#### Q. How is this different from a bookkeeping service?

Bookkeeping services enter numbers. We redesign your entire accounting infrastructure to produce numbers your management can actually use — design, operations, and financial analysis, all in one.

#### Q. Do I need to change my tax accountant?

No. Keep your current tax accountant. We handle daily accounting and monthly close; tax filings are coordinated with your accountant.

#### Q. We have a lot of paper receipts. Can you handle that?

Yes. Paper is fine. AI reads, digitizes, and journals them. You don't need to change how you work.

#### Q. How much does it cost?

It depends on the service. AI Accounting System starts at approximately ¥2.5 million after subsidies. Monthly BPO starts at ¥250,000/month. AI Training works out to roughly ¥89,000 per person after subsidies. See each service page for details.

#### Q. Can I use government subsidies?

The AI Accounting System is eligible for the Digital / AI Adoption Subsidy (50%, up to ¥4.5 million). AI Training is eligible for the Human Resources Development Subsidy (up to 75% for SMEs). We support the application process.

#### Q. What areas do you serve?

We work online, nationwide.

#### Q. How quickly can we start?

Start with a free consultation. We'll assess your current state and propose the fastest path forward.
