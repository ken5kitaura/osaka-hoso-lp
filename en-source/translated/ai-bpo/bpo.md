### [section #faq] FAQ
- **eyebrow**: FAQ

FAQ

#### Q. Can we use the BPO without the system implementation?

Talk to us. That said, the BPO delivers the most value when running on a properly rebuilt system.

#### Q. Why isn't this subsidy-eligible?

Subsidies cover tool implementation (the system build). Ongoing monthly operations are not eligible. The implementation step is where subsidies apply.

#### Q. How is this different from a tax accountant?

Tax accountants focus on filings. We handle daily operations and provide management-grade financial materials — filings are coordinated with your existing tax accountant.
