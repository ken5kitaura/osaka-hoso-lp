We rebuild your company's accounting as a single, end-to-end pipeline — from entry to output. This is not a tool implementation. It's a full replacement of your accounting function: from "it only works when that one person is here" to "anyone can run it and get the same result."

- **eyebrow**: FAQ

FAQ

#### Q. How is this different from cloud accounting software?

Software gives you a box. We rebuild your entire accounting system — from data entry to management metrics — and hand it over working.

#### Q. We have a lot of paper receipts. Is that a problem?

Not at all. AI reads, digitizes, and journals them. You don't need to change how you currently work.
